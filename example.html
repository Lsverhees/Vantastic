One Pan Wonders

About the Product

Vantastic offers One Pan Wonders, a cookbook designed for van life, featuring delicious recipes that require just one pan.

It simplifies cooking on the road by highlighting local ingredients, making every trip more memorable through the exploration of regional flavors.

With easy-to-follow recipes using a small number of fresh, local ingredients, One Pan Wonders provides a unique and practical way to cook in a camper van.
